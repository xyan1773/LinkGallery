为什么开发 LinkGallery？ Why build LinkGallery?

LinkGallery 的诞生源于对数据安全与原生体验的极致追求。市面上的云盘同步不仅缓慢且会压缩画质，而现有的相册管理工具又常常因误操作导致手机端的珍贵数据被意外删除。

我们重构了照片备份的逻辑：手机端数据受到绝对保护。 我们在 Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose) 上构建了严格的只读路由边界，不提供任何删除、移动或修改接口。所有复杂的备份状态管理，全权交由性能强劲的 Windows (.NET 8 / WPF) 客户端完成。

针对创作者的特殊需求，如 DJI Pocket 3 拍摄的素材，在 MVP 阶段只需通过 Mimo App 导入手机，LinkGallery 即可在局域网内无缝发现、预览并极速接管至电脑，打造完整且安全的创作链路。

LinkGallery was born out of a pursuit for data security and native experiences. Cloud syncing is slow and often compresses media, while existing gallery managers risk accidental deletion of precious memories.

We rebuilt backup logic: Your phone data is absolutely protected. We've built strict read-only boundaries on Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose), providing zero endpoints for deletion or modification. All complex backup state management is handled exclusively by the powerful Windows (.NET 8 / WPF) client.

For creators using devices like the DJI Pocket 3, simply sync to your phone via the Mimo App, and LinkGallery will instantly discover, preview, and take over the transfer to your PC entirely over your local network—creating a complete and safe creative workflow.

局域网自动发现 LAN Auto-Discovery

基于 mDNS / DNS-SD 协议。只需处于同一 Wi-Fi 网络，Windows 即可自动发现您的手机，无需繁琐的扫码或插拔数据线。 Powered by mDNS / DNS-SD. Simply connect to the same Wi-Fi, and Windows automatically discovers your phone. No more QR codes or USB cables.

断点续传与完整校验 Reliable Resumable Transfers

底层采用 HTTP Range 技术。即便是几个G大小的 4K 视频，也支持断点续传。传输完成后严格校验，拒绝任何损坏的“半截文件”。 Built on HTTP Range technology. Large 4K videos support resumable downloads, with strict post-transfer validation to prevent corrupted "half-downloaded" files.

绝对安全：只读保护 Strictly Read-Only Guarantee

手机端的 MediaStore 接口完全只读，不提供任何写入权限。所有的照片浏览与复制操作，都不会对您手机里的原数据产生一丝一毫的影响。 The Android MediaStore interface is completely read-only. Browsing and copying photos will never alter, move, or delete original data on your phone.
