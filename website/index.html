LinkGallery 是一个 local-first 的 Windows 统一终端，让您在一个界面中聚合浏览、使用自然语言搜索，并可靠传输来自所有个人设备的照片与视频。 LinkGallery is a local-first Windows photo terminal that unifies media from multiple personal devices and lets users find and transfer photos using natural language.

为什么开发 LinkGallery？ Why build LinkGallery?

现代人的照片管理极度割裂：Android 有自己的图库协议，iPhone 依赖 Apple 生态，DJI Pocket 3 有专门的 App，相机依靠 SD 卡，而 Windows 本地照片又散落在不同文件夹。每个平台的搜索、预览和导出方式都截然不同。

我们要解决的不是“如何把照片从手机传到电脑”，而是“如何通过一个一致的界面管理所有个人设备的照片？”

因此我们设计了核心平台 + 设备适配器（Adapters）的统一架构。Windows UI 不需要关心照片来自哪里，它只对接统一的 IMediaSource 接口。这使得跨设备聚合浏览与全局 AI 搜索成为可能。

Photo management today is highly fragmented: Android has its own protocols, iPhone relies on Apple's ecosystem, DJI requires specific apps, cameras use SD cards, and Windows local photos are scattered. Every platform has a completely different way to search, preview, and export.

We aren't just solving "how to copy photos to a PC". We are solving "how to manage photos across all personal devices through one consistent interface".

LinkGallery introduces a Core Platform + Device Adapters architecture. The Windows UI doesn't care where photos come from; it talks to a unified IMediaSource interface. This makes true cross-device aggregation and global AI search possible.

跨设备统一终端 Unified Interface

左侧同时显示本地 PC 和各种智能设备。用户在一个界面聚合浏览多个来源的照片，按时间、文件类型或设备进行全局筛选。 Browse "This PC" and your phone side-by-side. View photos from multiple sources in a single aggregated timeline, and filter by date, type, or device.

自然语言全局搜索 (AI) Natural Language Search

"找一下上周末在手机和电脑里的所有美食照片"。AI 会解析意图，自动在所有已连接设备中统一检索，并标明来源。 "Show me all photos of food taken last weekend across my phone and computer." AI translates this into a structured query across all connected adapters.

可插拔适配器架构 Pluggable Adapters

底层抽象统一接口。除了 Android 和 Windows，未来可轻松扩展 DJI、iPhone、SD 卡或 NAS 作为标准数据源。 Built on a foundational source interface. Future-proof and extensible for DJI, iPhone, SD cards, and NAS as standardized data sources.

极客级可靠传输 Reliable Local Transfer

跨设备复制照片时支持断点续传与完备的文件校验，杜绝损坏文件。同时设备源端始终保持只读，保障绝对安全。 Copy photos across devices with resumable transfer support and post-transfer validation. Source devices remain strictly read-only.
